# 🦖 Dino Gunner (Prototype)

> **An in-development browser-based survival shooter where you play as a heavily armed dinosaur.**

Take control of a fearless dinosaur and defend yourself against relentless waves of attacking pterodactyls. Collect coins, unlock stronger weapons, and survive for as long as you can.

⚠️ **This is an early prototype.** Dino Gunner is actively being developed, with new enemies, weapons, gameplay mechanics, visual improvements, and additional content planned for future updates.

## 🎮 Features

* 🦖 Play as an armed dinosaur
* 🦅 Survive waves of attacking pterodactyls
* 🔫 Unlock and use different weapons
* 💰 Collect coins during gameplay
* ❤️ Health and lives system
* 🌐 Play instantly in your browser
* ⚡ Fast-paced survival gameplay

## 🚧 Roadmap

Planned features include:

* 🦅 New enemy types
* 👑 Boss battles
* 🔫 More weapons
* 🗺️ New maps
* 🎵 Background music and improved sound effects
* 💥 Better visual effects
* 🏆 Achievements
* ⚖️ Gameplay balancing
